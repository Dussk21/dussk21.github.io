Recipes Page

Welcome to our Recipes Page, featuring a collection of healthy and sustainable recipes to inspire your culinary adventures.

Featured Recipes:

  1. Seasonal Vegetable Frittata

    Ingredients: Eggs, seasonal vegetables, cheese (optional), herbs.

    Instructions: Whisk eggs, sauté vegetables, pour egg mixture into skillet, bake until set.

  2. Quinoa Salad with Roasted Vegetables

    Ingredients: Quinoa, seasonal vegetables, olive oil, salt, pepper, vinaigrette.

    Instructions: Roast vegetables, cook quinoa, toss with vinaigrette, serve warm or chilled.

  3. Vegan Black Bean Chili

    Ingredients: Black beans, tomatoes, onion, garlic, bell pepper, spices.

    Instructions: Sauté veggies, add beans, tomatoes, spices, simmer, serve with toppings.

  4. Banana Oatmeal Breakfast Cookies

    Ingredients: Bananas, oats, nuts, dried fruit, maple syrup, vanilla, salt.

    Instructions: Mix ingredients, form cookies, bake until golden brown.

  5. Dark Chocolate Avocado Mousse

    Ingredients: Avocado, cocoa powder, maple syrup, vanilla, salt.

    Instructions: Blend ingredients until smooth, chill, garnish with berries or chocolate.
